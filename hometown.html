Welcome To My Hometown Page!

My home town is Dar es salaam in Tanzania. It is known for its beautiful tourist attractions such as Coco Beach, Dar es salaam Port, and Museums



Coco Beach:This is the most famous beach in Dar es salaam, it has great atmosphere were you can relax and enjoy the weather, surroundings and delicious food are available to make your day well spent.



Dar es salaam Port: This is the most biggest port in East Africa and it is found in Dar es salaam most of the goods transported by sheeps are destinated there. hence the surrounding is full of goods containers but it is a great place to explore since it is along the Indian Ocean



National Museum: It is the historical place found in Makumbusho Dar es salaam, it is an historical site with full of historical memories.
